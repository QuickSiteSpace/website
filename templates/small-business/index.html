Our story

Sunset Bakery started in 2014 as a weekend hobby in Marta and Theo's home kitchen. A few too many loaves later, they rented a tiny storefront on Grand Avenue and never looked back.

Today a small crew of bakers, pastry chefs, and very patient baristas keep the ovens warm seven mornings a week. We believe in slow fermentation, real butter, good neighbors, and saying yes to the kids who want to watch the mixer spin.
